About us

Adavin electronics is a shop that sells all electronic devices and components. Besides sell electronics, AdaVin also repair electronic devices, like all home gadgets, computers, phones and many more.

The name AdaVin came from two names, ADAC and VINICENT who are the founders of the AdaVin electronics.
